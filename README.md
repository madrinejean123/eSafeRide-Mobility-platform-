**eSafeRide Mobility Platform**

eSafeRide is a co-designed mobility solution aimed at improving transportation for students with disabilities at Makerere University. The platform addresses critical challenges such as limited accessible transport, unsafe informal boda boda services, lack of coordination, and unreliable travel options on campus. By integrating vetted boda boda operators, university fleet management, and real-time tracking, eSafeRide ensures safer, more reliable, and accessible mobility for students with disabilities.

**Challenges Addressed**

Scarce and inefficient accessible transport for students with disabilities

Safety risks from unregulated boda boda services

Lack of real-time trip tracking and emergency response

Poor route planning and resource management
**
Software Stack**

Mobile Apps: Flutter (for both student and driver apps)

Backend / Realtime: Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Messaging)

Admin Dashboard: Flutter Web or Firebase-hosted panel
